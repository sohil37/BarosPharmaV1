About Us

We are a well-known wholesaler of the quality approved assortment of Pharma Products. These offered ranges are admired in the market for their high purity and effectiveness.

Incorporated in the year 2021, We BAROS Pharma, have uniquely positioned ourselves as the prominent wholesaler of an exceptional quality assortment of Pharma Products. Offered products range consists of Anti-Cancer Drugs, Anti-Cancer Medication and Nephrology Drugs. Our vendors use high-quality ingredients to prepare this range. Apart from this, these products are highly appreciated due to their features like perfect packaging, accurate composition, and high effectiveness.


We become a famous firm with the help of our qualified team of professionals. Our vendors work dedicatedly to develop newer and innovative formulations to cater to the diverse requirements of the market. Together with this, the offered products are available in various packaging options in order to meet the exact requirements of clients. Our customized packaging options and affordable rates also help us in attaining high patron satisfaction.

Our Vision

Our company envisages playing a major role in the Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Industry in India by providing a uniquely integrated distribution and marketing solution from Point of supply (POS) to Last point of delivery (LPOD).

The company especially looks forward to distribution of Specialty Medicines in India by adopting global distribution standards. Company intends to develop special B2B portal for Pharmaceutical business.

Our Mission

Since 2021 professionals at BAROS Pharma have always striven to maintain highest quality standards. Customer & Supplier satisfaction have been the company’s top-most priorities.

Over the next decade, the company intends to extend its branch network for distribution of Super Specialty Drugs to Southern and Eastern Maharashtra in the short term and pan India in the long term period. Within the ambit of the stated laws of Government of India, the company also intends to start online retail operations in the near future using modern day mediums.

We believe, the online operations will provide a cost-effective solution for rising medicine costs and help patients make an informed choice. The company’s USP of Right Time, Right Price, Right Availability and Right Quality must keep benefitting Cancer, HIV, Nephrology & Virology Patients across the globe.
